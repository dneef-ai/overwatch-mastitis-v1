## Rationale

CpCPSF3 is the target of AN7973, the most potent cidal anti-cryptosporidial compound with in vivo calf efficacy data (>99% oocyst reduction). A co-crystal structure exists for the related benzoxaborole AN3661 bound to CpCPSF3 (Swale et al. 2019, Science Translational Medicine), but the binding mode of AN7973 differs — evidenced by the fact that the Y385N resistance mutation that confers strong resistance to AN3661 does NOT affect AN7973 sensitivity.

An AF3 co-folding prediction of CpCPSF3 with AN7973 would:
1. Identify the specific binding mode of AN7973 (distinct from AN3661)
2. Predict resistance-conferring residues specific to AN7973
3. Guide medicinal chemistry optimization for veterinary formulation
4. Provide structural basis for understanding the broader selectivity of 6-carboxamide benzoxaboroles
