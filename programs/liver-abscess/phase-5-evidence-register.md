## How to Read This Register

Each entry contains:
- **Claim:** The specific factual assertion
- **DOI/PMID:** Primary reference
- **Evidence tier:** ESTABLISHED (multiple independent replications, consensus) / MODERATE (peer-reviewed, limited replication) / PRELIMINARY (single study, preprint, or conference) / INFERRED (logical extrapolation from related data)
- **Species:** The species in which the claim was demonstrated
- **Replication:** Independent replication status
- **Key finding:** What the data actually shows
